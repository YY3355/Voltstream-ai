## Critical intel: Gridmatic is your benchmark

Gridmatic achieved $113/kW/year in ERCOT H1 2024, which was 61% above the $70/kW/year ERCOT average. They manage 1 GWh+ of storage. They use AI/ML for price forecasting and battery optimization.

This means:
- The market VALIDATES that AI optimization works (they proved it)
- Your differentiation needs to be clear (what do you do better/different?)
- Possible angles: more transparent pricing, better ancillary service co-optimization, hardware-agnostic (they may be locked to certain partners), simpler onboarding, performance guarantees

When operators ask "how are you different from Gridmatic?" you need an answer. Think about this over the weekend.
